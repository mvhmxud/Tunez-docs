# Welcome to Tunez Docs 

**Tunez** is a lightweight and customizable **React audio player Component** designed to make music playback smooth and stylish. Whether you're building a music streaming app, a podcast platform, or just need an embedded audio player, Tunez gives you full control over the UI and behavior.

## 🚀 Features

✅ **Modern & Minimal** – A sleek, responsive audio player.  
✅ **Fully Customizable** – Change themes, controls, and behavior.  
✅ **Lightweight & Fast** – Optimized for performance.  
✅ **Works with Any React App** – Easy to integrate in your project.
